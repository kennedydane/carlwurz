Carl Wurz

Counselling Psychologist

I am a counselling psychologist working in Port Alfred.  I also have a practice in Grahamstown on Wednesdays.  I work with clients of all ages, including children (play therapy).  I qualified as a Counselling Psychologist at Rhodes University and am registered with the Health Professionals Council of South Africa. I have a holistic approach to therapy.   Whereas my therapeutic modality is principally psychoanalytic, I also combine aspects of physiology, neuroscience and mindfulness when working with clients. I also do neuropsychological and career assessments for children and adults. I am fluent in both English and Afrikaans.
